## Product Purpose

Mental math practice tool tuned for quant interview formats. Adaptive algorithm surfaces weak skills, tracks speed and accuracy per skill, projects an Optiver 80-in-8 score. Cross-device sync via Supabase. Success = user can walk into an Optiver test and hit 70+.

## Brand Personality

Professional · Calm · Measured. Like a private tutor who knows exactly what's wrong and tells you without drama. Trustworthy over flashy. Every element earns its place.

## Anti-references

- **Mobile game UI** — no heavy gradients, glow effects, particle animations, or XP bars. This is not Duolingo.
- **Academic / textbook** — not plain white backgrounds with zero design intent. This should feel like a serious tool, not a PDF.
- **Generic SaaS** — flat white cards, blue primary buttons, startup-kit energy.

## Design Principles

1. **Data over decoration** — numbers and charts carry the design; ornament is suspicious.
2. **Calm confidence** — nothing shouts. Color signals state (green/amber/red), not excitement.
3. **Speed is the product** — the drill screen must feel instant; zero friction between questions.
4. **One task per screen** — home picks a mode, drill runs the drill, dashboard coaches. No blending.
5. **Trust the user** — no tooltips explaining obvious things, no onboarding wizards, no empty-state cheerleading.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Custom numpad with `inputMode="none"` to suppress mobile keyboard. Reduced motion should be respected for any future animations. Color coding always paired with text labels (not color-only signals).
